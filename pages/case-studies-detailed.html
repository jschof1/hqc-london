Mrs. Dean contacted us about her flat in Chelsea, she was planning renovation works and needed the space cleaned ahead of the builders arriving. She wasn't based in London, but she knew the property needed attention and wanted a professional clean to prepare the space.

When we visited the flat for an initial inspection, it became clear the condition was worse than expected. We filmed a full walkthrough and sent her a video report. She was shocked.

"Please do what you can," she said.

We got to work immediately cleaning top to bottom, removing multiple bags of rubbish, restoring order, and making the space workable for the incoming tradespeople. Once the clean was complete, we sent her another video walkthrough so she could see the transformation for herself. Her response said it all.

That first experience built a foundation of trust.

After the renovations were complete, she called us back for a post-build clean, and from there, we've continued to support her with regular maintenance cleans, window cleaning, laundry coordination, and anything else her property needs.

More recently, she asked us to visit a second property she was renting not just to clean, but to give an honest view on its condition and offer guidance on ongoing issues with the landlord. She knew she could rely on us not just to clean, but to advise.

For Mrs. Dean, we became more than a service, we became her trusted team on the ground.
