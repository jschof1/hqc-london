Optimization Actions I'm Implementing

Week 1
Homepage Conversion Enhancement
Refining the homepage to better capture those 237 visitors who are landing there
  • Sharpening the hero section value proposition
  • Strengthening calls-to-action with more prominent placement
  • Adding trust signals (insurance, testimonials, credentials) above the fold
  • Testing different headline variations
Expected: Reduce bounce rate by 15-20%, increase quotes by 25%
Week 2
Mobile Experience Overhaul
Only 12.3% of visitors are on mobile - huge opportunity to capture busy professionals
  • Comprehensive mobile UX audit and improvements
  • Optimizing tap targets and form fields
  • Improving mobile page load speeds
  • Ensuring WhatsApp integration works seamlessly
Expected: Unlock 30-40% more traffic from professionals on-the-go
Week 2-3
Service Page Conversion Optimization
Focusing on Services (41 views), Case Studies (21), and Contact (21) pages
  • Creating clear pathways between related pages
  • Adding "Next Steps" sections to guide visitors
  • Implementing strategic internal linking
  • Building dedicated landing pages for high-intent searches
Expected: Increase quote request rate from service pages by 35%
Week 3
Analytics & Tracking Enhancement
Currently no event tracking is set up - this is crucial for understanding conversions
  • GA4 event tracking for form submissions
  • Button click tracking (all CTAs)
  • Phone number tap and WhatsApp initiation tracking
  • Meta Pixel for future campaigns
  • Custom conversion dashboard
Expected: Clear visibility into conversion actions and bottlenecks
Week 4
Geographic & Audience Refinement
310 Singapore visitors (41% of traffic) are skewing our data and insights
  • Setting up geographic filters to focus on UK traffic
  • Creating region-specific performance reports
  • Implementing IP filtering to remove bot traffic
  • Building location-based landing pages for target areas
Expected: Clearer data showing actual UK client behaviour
Ongoing
Traffic Spike Analysis
October 16th generated 901 pageviews - let's understand and replicate this
  • Investigating what drove the October 8th, 16th, and 21st spikes
  • Identifying sources (social, referrals, PR)

  • Creating playbook to replicate successful patterns
